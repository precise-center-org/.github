# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

The Precise Center is committed to fostering a collaborative, respectful, and inclusive research community.

### We expect users and contributors to:

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Welcome diverse perspectives and expertise levels
- Give credit appropriately and acknowledge contributions
- Focus on advancing precision rehabilitation science
- Communicate openly and professionally

### Unacceptable behaviors include:

- Harassment, discriminatory language, or personal attacks
- Misrepresenting authorship or contributions
- Sharing others' unpublished work without permission
- Violating confidentiality agreements

## Contributor Responsibilities

### Permissions and Agreements

**Contributors are responsible for:**

- ✅ Obtaining all necessary **institutional approvals** before sharing resources
- ✅ Securing **co-author permissions** for any collaborative work
- ✅ Ensuring **agreement from all collaborators** on sharing and licensing
- ✅ Verifying **data sharing permissions** and privacy compliance
- ✅ Respecting **intellectual property** rights and existing agreements

**Before contributing, confirm that you have the right to share your resource under an open license.**

## Reporting Concerns

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please contact:

**Email:** PreciseCenter@kennedykrieger.org  
**Subject:** "Resource Core: Code of Conduct Concern"

All reports will be handled confidentially.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:
- Private warning
- Temporary suspension of privileges from the Precise Center GitHub account
- Permanent removal from the Precise Center GitHub account
- Reporting to appropriate institutional authorities
